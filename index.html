Philosophy
Good UX is not about beautiful screens — it is about decisions that survive contact with reality.

I've spent 14+ years working on products that handle money, identity, and trust at scale. How do you make accessibility non-negotiable rather than aspirational? How do you create systems that distributed teams actually adopt? These are the questions that drive my practice.
