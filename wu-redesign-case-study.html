WU E1 Redesign

I led the biggest redesign in Western Union’s history — unifying 5 fragmented digital products into one wallet-based platform. Managing a team of 11 designers across iOS, Android, and web, I balanced global brand consistency with regulatory compliance, accelerated delivery through dual-track agile, and introduced design QA rituals that reduced engineering rework by 40%.

Process

The project began with a 2-day product strategy workshop bringing together Product, Business, and Engineering leadership to align on vision before any design work began. This was followed by competitor IA analysis (Wise, Remitly, existing WU R4), card sorting with real users, and a complete service blueprint mapping the sender journey end-to-end.

Epic prioritisation using the RICE framework established a clear delivery roadmap. I championed building the Nexus Design System before production screens — a non-negotiable decision that made 142+ screens ship at consistent quality. Dual-track agile ensured discovery always ran ahead of delivery, eliminating the engineering bottlenecks that had plagued previous WU projects.
