Case Study — Western Union

Biggest overhaul in the history of Western Union

Unified 5 fragmented digital products into one modern, wallet-based platform. Led a team of 11 designers across iOS, Android, and web — balancing regulatory compliance, global brand consistency, and delivery velocity.

Year
2023 – Present
Impact
40% rework reduction via design QA
~5% drop-off reduction on key journeys
~20% faster design-to-engineering handoff

The Challenge
Legacy & Debt
Win leadership buy-in to challenge technical constraints, not design around them.
Alignment at Scale
Keep product, engineering, and design moving in the same direction across squads.
Compliance Without Friction
Make KYC/AML flows feel transparent and trustworthy despite heavy regulation.
Payments Hub

New entry point built around sending to a person, not selecting a corridor. Recent contacts, quick actions, and history as the primary interface.

Transfer Flow

Amount entry, live conversion, payment method, and fee transparency consolidated into one screen — reducing perceived steps from 7 to 3.

End-to-End Journey

19-screen bank transfer baseline covering entry through confirmation and error states — the definitive engineering handoff reference across all markets.

How we got there

Strategy first. 2-day workshop with Product, Business, and Engineering before a single screen was designed. Followed by competitor IA analysis (Wise, Remitly, WU R4) and card sorting to find the optimal information architecture.

Design system before production. Championed and won a dedicated Nexus Design System effort before any production screens — the decision that let 142+ screens ship at consistent quality.

Dual-track agile. Kept discovery running ahead of delivery at all times, eliminating the engineering bottlenecks that had defined previous WU projects. Design QA rituals cut rework by 40%.
